

# Conversion Landing Page

**High-Level Design & Section Documentation (Aligned to Approved Sketch)**

## Overview

This document describes the **exact page structure and section order** as shown in the provided hand-drawn design.
The layout is vertical, linear, and optimized for **conversion-focused landing pages**.

The intent is clarity, symmetry, and predictable flow — not experimentation.

---

## 1. Header + Hero Section (Combined at Top)

### Purpose

Introduce the brand and immediately present the core message.

### Layout (As per Sketch)

* The **header and hero visually sit together** at the top.
* The section spans the full content width.

### Header Elements

* **Company Logo**

  * Positioned at the top-left.
* **Navigation Bar**

  * Positioned horizontally across the top.
  * Links point to sections further down the same page.

### Hero Content

* **Main Heading**

  * Centered horizontally.
* **Subheading / Supporting Text**

  * Placed directly below the main heading.
* **Call to Action**

  * Center-aligned button or element below the text.
* **Symmetry**

  * Content is centrally aligned.
  * No full-screen hero; height is controlled and compact.

---

## 2. Problems Section

### Purpose

Clearly state the problems faced by the target audience.

### Placement

* Directly below the hero section.

### Content

* Section title: **Problems**
* Short explanation clarifying whether the problems are:

  * General, or
  * Niche-specific

### Layout

* Single block of content.
* Text-focused.
* No complex visuals.

---

## 3. Solutions Section (Grid)

### Purpose

Present solutions in a structured and scannable way.

### Layout (Exactly as Sketched)

* **Six solution boxes**

  * **Three boxes in the first row**
  * **Three boxes in the second row**
* Boxes are evenly spaced and aligned.

### Each Box Contains

* Short title
* One- or two-line description

### Notes

* Uniform sizing is mandatory.
* This section visually signals “capabilities” or “features”.

---

## 4. Brand Solution Section

### Purpose

Explain the primary solution or offering the brand stands for.

### Layout

* Two-column structure:

  * **Left:** Text content
  * **Right:** Image or visual placeholder

### Content

* Section heading: **Brand Solution**
* Short descriptive text explaining:

  * What the brand does
  * How it solves the problem

### Notes

* This section is more narrative than the grid above.
* It provides context and depth.

---

## 5. Pricing Plans Section

### Purpose

Allow users to compare plans and make a decision.

### Layout

* **Three pricing cards aligned horizontally**

  * Starter
  * Business
  * Enterprise

### Pricing Plan Details

#### Starter Plan

* Contains **3 features**
* Entry-level offering

#### Business Plan

* Contains **5 features**
* Mid-tier offering

#### Enterprise Plan

* Contains **7 features**
* Most comprehensive offering

### Design Notes

* All three cards have equal height.
* Each card includes:

  * Plan name
  * Feature list
  * Call-to-action button

---

## 6. Testimonials Section

### Purpose

Build trust and social proof.

### Layout

* Horizontal testimonial area.
* **Three testimonials visible at a time**.

### Content

* Testimonials may be AI-generated.
* Each testimonial includes:

  * Short quote
  * Name or identifier

### Functionality

* Right-arrow control present.
* Clicking the arrow cycles testimonials infinitely.
* Carousel loops continuously without an end.

---

## 7. Footer Section

### Purpose

Provide company information and secondary navigation.

### Layout (Three Lanes)

#### Left Lane

* Company logo or name
* Link back to the company website

#### Middle Lane

* Navigation links
* Mirrors main page sections

#### Right Lane

* Company details:

  * Company name
  * Address
  * Phone number

### Bottom Row

* Terms & Conditions
* Privacy Policy

---

## Overall Characteristics (As Intended by the Sketch)

* Vertical, single-page layout
* Clear section separation
* Symmetry and alignment prioritized
* No unnecessary sections
* Designed strictly for conversion landing pages
* Matches the provided drawing section-by-section
